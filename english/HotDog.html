For the Filling:

1/4 cup very finely minced onion 1 small clove garlic, finely chopped 1 teaspoon ground coriander 1 teaspoon sweet paprika 1/2 teaspoon ground mustard seed 1/4 teaspoon dried marjoram 1/4 teaspoon ground mace 1/4 cup milk 1 large egg white 1 1/2 teaspoons sugar 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste 1 teaspoon freshly ground white pepper 1 pound lean pork, cubed 3/4 pound lean beef, cubed 1/4 pound pork fat, cubed

For the Casing:

4 feet sheep casings (or small hog casings), about 1 1/2 inches in diameter 1 tablespoon white vinegar
